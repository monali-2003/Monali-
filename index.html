Welcome to the Solapur Institute for the Blind

The Solapur Institute for Blind is a leading institution dedicated to providing comprehensive training, support, and opportunities for individuals with visual impairments. Our mission is to empower and enable individuals to overcome challenges and achieve their fullest potential in a society that values inclusivity and equal opportunities.

We have created a network of Vision Rehabilitation and Education centers throughout India that currently Enable over 20,000 underprivileged individuals who are visually impaired and blind through a comprehensive range of assistive tools and devices. We Educate them with holistic training programs that increase their literacy, mobility, independence, and marketable job skills, and we Empower them with counseling and guidance that increases their confidence and motivation to pursue their ambitions.

Our USA team consists of over fifty Ophthalmology, Optometry, Software, Education, Finance and Business uncompensated volunteer professionals that are supported by a highly talented front-line organization in India. Almost all the funds raised from our gracious donors go directly to help the visually impaired in India. We plan to grow our support by 30% per year in the coming 2 years through partnerships, valuable services and optimizing operational efficiencies and costs.

Our collaborative Resource Centers partners are among India’s top 5 rated institutions such as the Aravind Eye Hospital at Madurai, Sankara Nethralaya at Chennai and Kolkata, L.V. Prasad Eye Institute Hyderabad, Dr. Shroff’s Charity Eye Hospital at Delhi, Voluntary Health Services Hospital in Chennai and Community Eye Care Foundation (CECF) in Pune. These SIB Resource centers provide low vision assessment, assistive devices and training, orientation and mobility training services, affordable and innovative technology such as electronic digital magnifiers, breakthrough Artificial Intelligence Smart Vision Glasses and computer education among others. The Online, and Classroom education initiative run via the Vision-Aid Academy increases the literacy, personal communication, mobility, independence, competitive entrance examinations and interviewing skills and soft skills to succeed in corporate jobs. Our Blind school initiative equips young students with computer skills and with innovative Tactile Braille Book

approved for FCRA by the Indian Ministry of Home Affairs.
