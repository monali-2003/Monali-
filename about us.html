About us

At the Solapur Institute for the Blind, we are dedicated to empowering individuals with visual impairments and promoting their independence. We believe that everyone, regardless of their visual abilities, deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow, and thrive in a supportive environment.

We have created a network of Vision Rehabilitation and Education centers throughout India that currently Enable over 20,000 underprivileged individuals who are visually impaired and blind through a comprehensive range of assistive tools and devices. We Educate them with holistic training programs that increase their literacy, mobility, independence, and marketable job skills, and we Empower them with counseling and guidance that increases their confidence and motivation to pursue their ambitions.

Our Vision

Our vision is a society that values and respects individuals with visual impairments, where they have equal access to education, employment, and social inclusion. We strive to create a world where visual impairments do not limit one's potential or hinder their participation in society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive training programs and support services to individuals with visual impairments. We aim to equip them with the necessary skills, knowledge, and confidence to overcome challenges and lead fulfilling lives. Through our courses and initiatives, we foster an inclusive and accessible society that embraces diversity.
